3.5.3   Gross Loan Portfolio Across States/UTs

The Gross Loan Portfolio (GLP) across states/ UT and year on year change is presented in Table 3.9. The top 5 states Y-o-Y growth, were Bihar (54%), Tamil Nadu (47%), Karnataka (33%), Uttar Pradesh (50%) and West Bengal (67%). Also riding on the Supreme Court on the microfinance ordinance of the erstwhile combined Andhra Pradesh, microlending institutions expanded their portfolio in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana rapidly with almost doubling the same.
