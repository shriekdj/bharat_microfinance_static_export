3.4.ii    Loan Portfolio of SFBs

Small Finance Banks were licensed in 2015-16 as a new initiative of RBI to reach the unreached sections with banking facility. Out of the 10 SFBs, licensed 8 were MLIs. Most of their loan portfolio is still dominated by microfinance loans. Thus, they are a key player in microfinance sector. All the 8 SFBs together had a loan portfolio of ₹58,153 Cr.
