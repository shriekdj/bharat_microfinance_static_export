3.3.3  State-wise Client Outreach

The States/UTs wise client base is mapped in Table 3.3. It is observed that all States/UTs have experienced a positive growth, except for Chandigarh, Manipur and Tripura. The major states in terms of portfolio which have experienced higher positive growth are Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand, West Bengal, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Odisha etc. Notably, Andhra Pradesh and Telangana have recorded significant increase in client outreach due to entry of several MLIs in these states after a favorable Court order.

The growth in base in Bihar and Uttar Pradesh recorded a sharp increase by 39% and 36%, respectively. As the states like Uttar Pradesh and Bihar have lower banking presence, this space is being filled by these MLIs, which will help in advancing the financial inclusion agenda of the country. Other major states in terms of number of clients under the fold of MLIs were Karnataka, Tamil Nadu and Madhya Pradesh which recorded 19%, 28% and 26% growth, respectively.
