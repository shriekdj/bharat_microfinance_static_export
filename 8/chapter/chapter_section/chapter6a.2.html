6A.2  Benefits of Parametric Insurance

    1. Rapid Pay-outs: Parametric insurance triggers payouts based on objective data, expediting the claims settlement process and ensuring faster access to funds for recovery.
    1. Transparency: The pre-defined triggers and payout structure ensure transparency in the insurance process.
    1. Complementary to Traditional Insurance: Parametric insurance can complement traditional indemnity-based insurance by covering losses not typically covered by traditional policies.
    1. Tailormade: Parametric insurance solutions are tailored to the customer’s specific exposures, risk appetite, and legal environment. With our team of experts and years of global experience, we are well- equipped to develop a customized product for the states.
