The above table indicates the region-wise and state-wise distribution of NBFC-MFIs. Although most of these MFIs have multiple state and region presence, the distribution is based on their headquarters. Table 3 A.2shows that South region has maximum number (28) of NBFC-MFIs having their headquarters located in this region.
