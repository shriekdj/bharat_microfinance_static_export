3.6.2   Rural – Urban Share of No. of Loans Disbursed

Out of nearly 554 lakhs loans disbursed by MLIs during FY 2023-24, around 410 lakh loans were disbursed in rural areas which constituted 74% of share. Correspondingly, ₹1,65,829 Cr. was disbursed in rural areas and ₹58,264 Cr. in urban areas during the year.
